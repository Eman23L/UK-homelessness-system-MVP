# Pilot Plan

## Pilot Area
Milton Keynes (initial test area)

## Goals
- Collect shelter locations
- Collect food bank locations
- Collect emergency support services

## Data Sources
- Local council homelessness pages
- National charities (Crisis, Salvation Army, YMCA)
- GiveFood API

## Success Criteria
- At least 10 verified service entries
- Data matches schema format
- Includes location and contact details
- Includes eligibility where available

## Notes
Start small and validate the process before scaling to other cities.
